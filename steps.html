Pumpmas Extension - How to Install

Follow these simple steps to install Pumpmas and start spreading holiday cheer!

  1. Download the extension: Click the “Download Pumpmas Extension” button on the main page.
  2. Unzip the file: Extract the contents to a folder on your computer.
  3. Open your browser's extensions page:
    • Chrome: chrome://extensions/
    • Edge: edge://extensions/
  4. Enable Developer Mode: Toggle the switch on the top right of the extensions page.
  5. Load unpacked extension: Click “Load unpacked” and select the folder where you extracted Pumpmas.
  6. Activate your extension: Make sure you hold at least 10,000 Pumpmas tokens to unlock full features.

Watch the Installation Video

Once installed, refresh Pump.fun and enjoy the festive Pumpmas features!
